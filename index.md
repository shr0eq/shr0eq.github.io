## About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at
**Seoul National University**, and a Student Researcher at the
**Center for Semiconductor Technology, Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST)**.

My current research focuses on spin-orbit torques, spin transport phenomena,
and magnonic effects in magnetic thin film heterostructures.
